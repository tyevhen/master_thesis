a recent gallup poll cited levels of well being in the usa research was done with over thousand americans and focused on levels of well being purpose , social , financial , community and physical below are more detailed definitions of these categories purpose well being is composed of questions about having an inspiring leader , daily activity , goals , and strengths social well being includes questions about relationships with friends and family , personal time , and received encouragement and support financial well being is made up of questions about standard of living , ability to afford basic necessities , and financial worry community well being includes questions about community pride , involvement , and safety and security physical well being includes questions related to alcohol , drug , and tobacco use current disease burden and past diagnoses exercise and eating habits gallup reports that half of americans are thriving in only one well being element or none at all and fewer than one in five u s adults are thriving in four or five elements , which indicates that many individuals are not experiencing well being when it comes to enhancing your own well being , remember that there are aspects you can and cannot control for example , while you may not be able to make more money to increase your financial well being , but you can improve your social , community , physical and purpose well being set realistic goals and make sure to appreciate the meaningful connections you experience
