how we think and what we believe can lead us down very different roads our state of mind is a very powerful phenomenon case in point , we all know what the placebo effect is where health benefits can be produced by a non real treatment but did you know there is a nocebo effect where something that is ineffective causes ill health ? while the placebo effect refers to health benefits produced by a treatment that should have no effect , patients experiencing the nocebo effect experience the opposite they presume the worst , health wise , and that 's just what they get not much is known about how the nocebo effect occurs , but theories of origin include biological and psychological factors for more on this , read here or this article in science daily
