instead of leaving them scattered around the house i brought them together in one place so i could see , at a glance , what needed to be done then , i wrote a to do list writing my tasks in time order helps me to work out the priority , for example i am speaking at two events , one on the st and the second on the th , i need a slightly different speech for both but the st event needs to be written first once i had everything written down and my diary and calendar were coordinated i could see the gaps in my time where the niggling worries could be slotted for example on tuesday i picked up a new
